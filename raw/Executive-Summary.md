# Agent Pekka: Executive Summary

## Company Overview
Agent Pekka is a globally respected illustration and visual arts agency founded in 2007. With offices in Helsinki, Amsterdam, Paris and Los Angeles, we represent approximately 50 exceptional visual artists, connecting them with the world's most iconic brands. Our agency has built a reputation for championing originality, newness, and craftsmanship within the creative industry.

## Current Position
- **Founded:** 2007
- **Ownership:** Fully owned by the core team
- **Revenue:** €3M in 2023, with a 4% profit margin
- **Clients:** Premium global brands including Apple and Louis Vuitton
- **Artists:** 50 established or rising visual talents
- **Global Presence:** Helsinki, Amsterdam, Los Angeles, Paris
- **Subsidiary:** Fount, a digital gallery trading physical artworks

## Market Opportunity
The global illustration market is valued at $16.5B in 2023 and projected to grow to $26.3B by 2030 (CAGR: 9.7%). Additionally, the stock images market is valued at $6.4B and expected to reach $12.2B by 2032. This substantial and growing market presents significant opportunities for a premium agency with an exceptional artist roster and global ambitions.

## Vision
To become the leading global agency where top-tier visual artists meet the world's most iconic brands, creating impactful creative collaborations while achieving €50M annual revenue with 10% profit margin within three years.

## Expansion Strategy
Our three-pronged approach to growth combines geographic expansion with diversification of revenue streams:

### 1. Global Expansion
- **Aggressive scaling** to 20 global cities by 2026
- **Systematic approach** to identifying optimal markets and founding agents
- **Proven model** demonstrating €700K-1.2M annual revenue potential per location
- **Initial focus** on Paris and Shanghai (2025), with 2-4 additional cities by fall 2025

### 2. Revenue Stream Diversification
- **Licensing Platform:** Monetizing our artists' back catalogs with a three-tiered approach:
  - High-end licenses (€50,000+) for exclusive, global, perpetual rights
  - Mid-tier licenses (€5,000-€25,000) for limited exclusivity 
  - Low-end licenses (€1,000+) through an automated platform for scale
  - Projected to generate €680K in year one, growing to €1.5M+ by year three

- **Game Art IP Incubation:** Developing and licensing visual IP for the gaming industry
  - Creating a sister company focused on game art concepts
  - Potential for substantial royalties and recurring revenue
  - Expected to begin generating revenue in 2025

- **Fount Gallery Enhancement:** Transforming our digital gallery into a robust exhibition and B2C platform
  - Supporting both physical and digital exhibitions
  - Potential for print-on-demand offerings from licensing portfolio
  - TV app galleries for broader reach

### 3. Technology Development
- **Licensing Portfolio Website:** Dedicated platform for showcasing and managing licensing operations
- **Scalable Toolset:** Development of comprehensive agent network tools
- **AI Toolset:** Style adaptation tools for layout creation and game world development
- **SaaS Licensing Platform:** Automated platform for bulk licensing (2026 launch)
