### **Project Prioritization Framework**

To ensure strategic implementation of our initiatives, we've developed a prioritization framework that evaluates each project according to five key criteria on a scale of 1-5 (5 being highest):

1. **Revenue Impact (RI)**: Potential to generate direct revenue in the near to medium term
2. **Strategic Value (SV)**: Alignment with long-term vision and ecosystem development
3. **Resource Requirement (RR)**: Inverse score - lower resource needs score higher (5 = minimal resources)
4. **Time to Value (TV)**: Speed at which initiative will deliver measurable results
5. **Dependency Factor (DF)**: Inverse score - fewer dependencies score higher (5 = no dependencies)

**Priority Score = (RI + SV + RR + TV + DF) / 5**

### **Risk Management Framework**

**High-Impact Risks:**

* **Expansion Pace Risk:** Expanding too rapidly could strain resources and dilute brand quality
  * **Mitigation:** Implement staged expansion with clear success metrics and pause points
* **Founding Agent Selection Risk:** Unsuitable agents could severely impact expansion success
  * **Mitigation:** Rigorous selection criteria, probationary periods, comprehensive training
* **Quality Control Risk:** Maintaining consistent standards across multiple locations
  * **Mitigation:** Centralized oversight, regular audits, standardized processes
* **Capital Requirements Risk:** Expansion may require more capital than anticipated
  * **Mitigation:** Detailed financial models, phased funding, multiple funding sources

**Medium-Impact Risks:**

* **Business Model Diversification Risk:** New revenue streams may not achieve projected returns
* **Competitive Response Risk:** Established agencies may respond aggressively to expansion
* **Technology Development Risk:** Platforms may take longer or cost more than anticipated
* **Economic Downturn Risk:** Global economic conditions could impact client budgets

**Risk Monitoring:**
* Quarterly risk assessment reviews
* Assignment of risk owners for high-priority risks
* Regular reporting on risk indicators to leadership team
* Annual comprehensive risk assessment update
