## Resource Allocation & Dependencies

### Key Dependencies
- Founding agent recruitment success determines expansion pace
- Licensing platform development enables scaling of licensing business
- AI model training infrastructure enables Pekka Worlds Studio scaling
- Artist roster growth supports all revenue initiatives
- Technology development enables operational efficiency at scale
- Paris gallery-restaurant funding and partnership success
- Brand collaboration program development and client acquisition

### Critical Resource Allocations
- **2025**: Focus on expansion foundation, technology infrastructure, initial licensing, Pekka Worlds Studio launch, brand collaborations, and Paris gallery-restaurant
- **2026**: Emphasis on scaling locations, SaaS platform launch, AI model licensing, and revenue diversification across all business units
- **2027**: Optimization of all channels, innovation, and potential new ventures including spin-offs

### Risk Factors & Contingencies
- **Expansion Pace**: If founding agent recruitment slows, shift resources to improving existing locations
- **Technology Development**: If SaaS platform development takes longer, prioritize high-end licensing manually
- **AI Model Development**: If AI model training faces technical challenges, focus on traditional visual style licensing
- **Paris Gallery-Restaurant**: If funding or partnership issues arise, consider pop-up or alternative venue models
- **Brand Collaborations**: If brand acquisition is slow, focus on existing client relationships and case study development
- **Market Conditions**: Adjust expansion pace based on economic conditions in target markets
- **Revenue Mix**: Shift resources to highest-performing channels if certain initiatives underperform

This roadmap serves as a living document to guide Agent Pekka's strategic implementation while maintaining flexibility to adapt to market conditions and opportunities.
