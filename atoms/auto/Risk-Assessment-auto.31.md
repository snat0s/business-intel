### Intellectual Property Risk
**Description**: Complex licensing arrangements could create IP disputes or vulnerabilities.  
**Impact**: High | **Likelihood**: Medium | **Rating**: High  
**Mitigation Strategies**:
- Engage specialized IP lawyers to develop contracts
- Create clear IP guidelines for all business lines
- Implement comprehensive rights management system
- Regular IP audits and compliance checks
