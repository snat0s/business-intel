From the outset, Agent Pekka’s mission was clear: *“to make Finnish illustration known internationally.”* This guiding goal was evident early on, as the founders assembled a hand-picked group of Finnish illustrators under one roof so they’d be easier for global clients to find ([Muotoilijat '11 Finnish Designer Awards by ORNAMO \- Issuu](https://issuu.com/ornamo/docs/muotoilijat11_web_7019b42551bf77#:~:text=The%20answer%20on%20the%20phone,digital%20image%20production%20and%20service)). By representing a roster of Finland’s top illustration talent, Agent Pekka sought to export the nation’s distinctive visual art style beyond its borders. In the agency’s early years, they focused on promoting originality and high-quality craft, principles that would become core values of the company ([Agent Pekka](https://www.gosee.news/agentpekka/page/about#:~:text=We%27re%20a%20global%20artist%20representation,Los%20Angeles%2C%20and%20work%20everywhere)).
