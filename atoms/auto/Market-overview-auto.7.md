**Market Size & Growth:** The market for creative representation and talent management in illustration and digital art is growing steadily. The **global Illustration Agency Service market** was valued around **$1.3 billion in 2023**, with forecasts reaching about **$2.6 billion by 2032** (CAGR \~7.5%) ([Illustration Agency Service Market Research Report 2032](https://dataintelo.com/report/illustration-agency-service-market#:~:text=The%20global%20Illustration%20Agency%20Service,advertising%2C%20publishing%2C%20and%20digital%20media)). Companies increasingly rely on **visual storytelling** in marketing, using illustration and digital art to capture attention on social media and digital platforms ([Illustration Agency Service Market Research Report 2032](https://dataintelo.com/report/illustration-agency-service-market#:~:text=One%20of%20the%20primary%20growth,visual%20identity%20and%20brand%20narratives)).
