5. **Workflow Digitalization and Automation** (Priority Score: 3.8)
   * RI: 3 | SV: 4 | RR: 4 | TV: 4 | DF: 4
   * Timeline: Q1-Q3 2025
   * **Benefits:**
     - Reduces administrative overhead
     - Enables efficient scaling without proportional cost increase
     - Creates consistent client experience across locations
     - Supports remote work and global coordination
   * **Dependencies:**
     - Requires process documentation and standardization
     - Needs training and adoption across all locations
     - May require integration with existing systems
   * **Expense:**
     - New licenses
     - Time for building and integrating
     - Onboarding of personnel
   * **Suggested KPIs:**
     1. Time saved on administrative tasks (target: 30% reduction)
     2. System adoption rate among staff (target: 90% active weekly users)
     3. Process standardization rate (target: 85% of workflows documented and automated)
