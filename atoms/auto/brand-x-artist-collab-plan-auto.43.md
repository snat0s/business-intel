* **Supreme x Artists (Various, 2000s–present)** – *Streetwear Licensing of Art*: Streetwear label Supreme built its cult status partly by collaborating with visual artists. The brand has released limited-edition t-shirts, skateboards, and accessories featuring work by Jean-Michel Basquiat, Damien Hirst, George Condo, KAWS, and others. In these deals, Supreme typically arranges a **licensing agreement** with the artist or their estate: the artist permits use of specific artworks on a limited product run, and in exchange receives a fee or royalty. For example, **Supreme x Basquiat** (2013) put Basquiat’s paintings on skate decks and shirts, and **Supreme x Damien Hirst** (2009) featured Hirst’s spot paintings on skateboards.
