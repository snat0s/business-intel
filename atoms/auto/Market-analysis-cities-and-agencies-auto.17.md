* **Europe:** Europe is another major region, likely contributing a similar magnitude as North America. Within Europe, **Western Europe** – especially the **UK, France, and Germany** – drives most of the demand. The UK (with London as a global illustration hub) is particularly prominent; it houses many of the world’s leading illustration agencies and a vibrant publishing industry. A recent UK survey by the Association of Illustrators collected data from 25 leading UK-based agencies, indicating a substantial agency market there. Continental Europe also has strong markets: **France** (centered in Paris, with its advertising, fashion, and ([AOI Publishes 2024 Pricing Survey Results](https://theaoi.com/news/2024-aoi-pricing-survey#:~:text=This%20comprehensive%20resource%20offers%20insights,across%2025%20UK%20illustration)) sectors), **Germany** (advertising and media in cities like Berlin, Hamburg, Munich), and others like **Italy** and the **Nordics** (Scandinavia has a notable design and illustration tradition). Europe’s share of the global illustration agency market was significant enough that multiple European agencies rank among top players globally.
