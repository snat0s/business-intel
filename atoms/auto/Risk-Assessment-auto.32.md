### Cross-Border Compliance Risk
**Description**: Operating across multiple jurisdictions creates complex compliance requirements.  
**Impact**: Medium | **Likelihood**: High | **Rating**: Medium  
**Mitigation Strategies**:
- Engage local legal counsel in each jurisdiction
- Create compliance checklist for new market entry
- Regular compliance reviews and updates
- Centralized tracking of all regulatory requirements
