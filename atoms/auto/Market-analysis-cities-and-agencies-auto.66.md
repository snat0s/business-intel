**4\. Los Angeles (USA):** *Entertainment Industry and Beyond.* Los Angeles is a major center due to Hollywood’s influence. The **entertainment industry** (film studios, TV networks, streaming services) relies on illustrators for **movie posters**, **show concept art**, **storyboards**, and marketing materials. Illustration agencies in LA often represent artists who do one-sheets (posters) or key art for entertainment campaigns. Additionally, LA’s advertising scene (serving tech, fashion, and automotive companies on the West Coast) commissions plenty of illustration. Many agencies headquartered elsewhere have satellite offices or reps in LA (for example, **IllustrationX** and **Agent Pekka** have Los Angeles contacts). There are also LA-based agencies like **Innovative Artists** (a talent agency that includes illustrators, estimated revenue \~$161M, though that includes broader talent representation) and boutique reps focusing on animation art. **Industries:** Film and TV marketing (a big source of high-budget commissions), video game developers (LA/SoCal has many game studios needing concept and marketing art), music industry (album cover illustrations, tour posters), and tech (Silicon Beach startups using illustration in branding). *Notable agencies:* **CIA** and **Jelly** have West Coast reps, \*\*Agent ([Agent pekka Reviews 2025: Profile Details | GoodFirms](https://www.goodfirms.co/company/agent-pekka#:~:text=,United%20States)) ice focusing on design/animation illustrators), **Shannon Associates** often works with LA publishers/studios, and **B\&A** has a Los Angeles presence as well.
