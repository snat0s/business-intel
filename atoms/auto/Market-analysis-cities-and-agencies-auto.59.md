* **IllustrationX (Illustration Ltd):** A leading global illustration agency (founded in the U.K., with offices in multiple countries). IllustrationX represents a large roster of artists (200+ across styles) and services clients worldwide in advertising, publishing, and branding. It is one of the largest agencies by revenue – estimated to generate around $50–65 million annually – reflecting its extensive operations. IllustrationX prides itself on a broad range of styles and has decades of experience (est. 1929), making it a go-to for many big brands and publishers.  
* **Advocate Art:** A major agency specializing in children’s book illustration and licensing (with offices in London, New York, etc.). Founded in 1992, Advocate Art has a large network of illustrators particularly for children’s books, educational art, and greeting cards. Its revenue is on the order of $5–10 million per year, and it has a mid-sized staff. Advocate’s strength is in publishing: it connects artists with book publishers, toy and game companies, and others needing family-friendly and educational imagery.  
* **Bernstein & Andriulli (B\&A):** A New York-based creative agency representing not only illustrators but also photographers, animators, and other creatives. B\&A is known globally in the advertising industry, often handling high-profile ad campaigns and editorial illustration commissions. Its illustration segment revenue is not publicly reported in detail, but estimates put B\&A’s overall revenue in the multi-million dollar range (roughly $5–15 million) based on available data. B\&A’s competitive edge is its diverse talent roster and deep ties to advertising agencies and corporate clients.  
* **Central Illustration Agency (CIA):** A London-based agency (est. 1983\) representing a wide array of illustrators and animators. CIA has a strong presence in Europe’s advertising and editorial market. While exact figures aren’t public, CIA is considered one of the prominent mid-to-large U.K. agencies, likely handling several million dollars in commissions annually. It is known for its long-standing reputation and a curated roster covering traditional and digital illustration styles.  
* **Handsome Frank:** A boutique illustration agency from the U.K. (founded by two brothers in 2010). Despite its smaller size, Handsome Frank has built an impressive client list in advertising and editorial design. It represents a select group of artists (around 40\) known for contemporary, design-forward styles. Handsome Frank’s revenue is smaller (a few million USD or less), but it has strong brand positioning in the creative industry for quality and trend-aware artwork.
