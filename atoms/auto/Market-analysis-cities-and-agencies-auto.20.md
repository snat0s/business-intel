* **Middle East & Africa:** These regions are the smallest in terms of the illustration market. However, there is growing use of illustration in advertising and media in the Middle East (e.g., Dubai’s a ([Norte](https://norte.art.br/#:~:text=Norte%20Norte%20is%20an%20artists,become%20experts%20in%20copyright%20management)) ([GERA Illustration Agency](https://gera.art.br/#:~:text=GERA%20Illustration%20Agency%20NEW%20WORKS,Uber%20Eats)) publishing in markets like Turkey). Africa’s illustration usage is relatively nascent on the global stage, mostly limited to local publishing and advertising. Combined, these regions likely constitute only a few percent of global illustration expenditures. The global market is still heavily concentrated in North America, Europe, and APAC.
