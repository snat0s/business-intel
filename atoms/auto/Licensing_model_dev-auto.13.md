### Assumptions
- **Initial Portfolio**: 1,000 pieces from 50 artists + 30 new pieces from new series = 1,030 licensable works.
- **Licensing Mix**:
  - 2 high-end licenses (avg. €75,000)
  - 8 mid-tier licenses (avg. €15,000)
  - 40 low-end licenses (avg. €1,000)
- **Sales Target**: 50 licenses in Year 1 (conservative), with 50% annual growth.
- **Revenue Split**: 50% to artists, 50% to Agent Pekka.
- **Operational Costs**: €75,000 in Year 1 (platform, marketing, legal), with moderate increases thereafter.
