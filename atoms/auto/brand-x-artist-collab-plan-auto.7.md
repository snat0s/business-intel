* **What It Is:** Instead of (or in addition to) royalties, the artist may receive a share of the profits from the product line. This model is less common but can be attractive if the product margins are high.  
* **Sums Involved:**  
  * **Earnings:** Highly variable, often tied directly to performance. Successful products can yield multi-million-dollar payouts, whereas lower-than-expected sales might result in modest sums.
