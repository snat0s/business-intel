### Founding Agent Selection Risk
**Description**: Selecting unsuitable founding agents could severely impact expansion success in new locations.  
**Impact**: High | **Likelihood**: Medium | **Rating**: High  
**Mitigation Strategies**:
- Develop rigorous selection criteria and assessment processes
- Implement probationary periods with clear performance metrics
- Create comprehensive training program for new agents
- Establish mentorship relationships with experienced agents
