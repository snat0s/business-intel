   * RI: 3 | SV: 4 | RR: 3 | TV: 3 | DF: 4
   * Timeline: Q2-Q4 2025
   * **Benefits:**

     - Generates content for marketing materials
     - Provides platform for artist development

     2. Gallery visitor-to-customer conversion rate (target: 5%)
