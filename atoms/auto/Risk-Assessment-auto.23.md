### Talent Acquisition Risk
**Description**: Difficulty finding suitable talent (agents, managers, specialists) could slow expansion.  
**Impact**: High | **Likelihood**: Medium | **Rating**: High  
**Mitigation Strategies**:
- Develop talent pipeline through industry networks
- Create appealing career progression opportunities
- Implement referral programs for high-quality candidates
- Consider developing talent internally through training programs
