It represents hundreds of artists. Estimates of Bright’s revenue vary – one source pegs it around **$47.8M** annually, though another ([Commercial Illustration Market To Witness the Highest Growth](https://www.openpr.com/news/3583543/commercial-illustration-market-to-witness-the-highest-growth#:~:text=Top%20key%20players%3A%20Illustration%20Web%2C,Uncle%2C%20Jelly%20London%2C%20Heart%20Agency)) modest $5.9M (the discrepancy might be between total turnover vs. UK-only filings). In any case, Bright is considered one of the largest agencies, notable for its focus on art for children’s media and its expansion into the U.S. market (they have a New York office). They recently even started a literary div ([Bright Agency: Revenue, Competitors, Alternatives \- Growjo](https://growjo.com/company/Bright_Agency#:~:text=Bright%20Agency%27s%20estimated%20annual%20revenue,per%20employee%20is%20%24267%2C300)) cating diversification.
