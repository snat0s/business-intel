### Development Timeline Risk
**Description**: Technology platforms (licensing platform, SaaS tools) may take longer or cost more than anticipated.  
**Impact**: Medium | **Likelihood**: High | **Rating**: Medium  
**Mitigation Strategies**:
- Implement agile development methodology with regular deliverables
- Develop MVP approach to launch core functionality first
- Partner with experienced development teams
- Create manual workarounds for critical functions during development
