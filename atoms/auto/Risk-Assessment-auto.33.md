### Contract Risk
**Description**: Complex artist and client agreements across jurisdictions could create contractual vulnerabilities.  
**Impact**: Medium | **Likelihood**: Medium | **Rating**: Medium  
**Mitigation Strategies**:
- Standardized contract templates with local customization
- Regular legal review of all contract types
- Clear approval processes for contract modifications
- Documentation of all contract negotiations
