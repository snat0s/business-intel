  **1\. Definition and Scope of the Illustration Market**  
    
  The illustration market encompasses all **commercial illustration work** that is **licensed or commissioned** for use by clients. This includes a broad range of sectors and purposes:  
* **Editorial illustration:** Artwork commissioned for newspapers and magazines.  
* **Book and publishing illustration:** Illustrations for book covers, children’s books, graphic novels, and educational publishing.  
* **Advertising and marketing visuals:** Visuals created for marketing campaigns, print ads, product packaging, and branding.  
* **Gaming and entertainment art:** Concept art, character design, storyboards, and promotional illustrations for video games, films, TV, and animation.  
* **Licensed digital art:** Illustration assets licensed for use in products or media (e.g., merchandise graphics, infographic assets, iconography, stock illustrations).
