* **Ensure Credit and Branding for the Artist**: A collaboration is also about the artist’s brand. Best practice is to agree on how the artist’s name or signature will appear on products and marketing. Whether it’s co-branding on labels or mentioning the artist in ads, this visibility not only is respectful, but can lead to long-term brand value for the artist. For example, in the Supreme x Basquiat collab, Supreme’s logo was prominent but it was clear Basquiat’s art was the focus (\[
