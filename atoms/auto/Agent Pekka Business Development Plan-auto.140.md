* **Expansion Pace Risk:** Expanding too rapidly could strain resources and dilute brand quality
  * **Mitigation:** Implement staged expansion with clear success metrics and pause points
* **Founding Agent Selection Risk:** Unsuitable agents could severely impact expansion success
  * **Mitigation:** Rigorous selection criteria, probationary periods, comprehensive training
* **Quality Control Risk:** Maintaining consistent standards across multiple locations
  * **Mitigation:** Centralized oversight, regular audits, standardized processes
* **Capital Requirements Risk:** Expansion may require more capital than anticipated
  * **Mitigation:** Detailed financial models, phased funding, multiple funding sources
