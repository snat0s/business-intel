1. **Licensing Business Model** (Priority Score: 4.6)
   * RI: 5 | SV: 5 | RR: 4 | TV: 5 | DF: 4
   * Timeline: Q1-Q2 2025
   * **Benefits:**
     - Monetizes existing artwork portfolio (currently untapped resource)
     - Creates passive revenue stream complementing commissioned work
     - Reduces dependency on new production work
     - Initial revenue potential: €50K-500K in first year
     - Addresses €6.4B stock images market, projected to reach €12.2B by 2032
   * **Dependencies:**
     - Requires new legal frameworks and artist contracts
     - Needs digital asset management system implementation
     - Requires marketing through gallery exhibitions and web platform
     - Needs content tagging and metadata standards
   * **Expense:**
     - Internal development time
     - Legal fees €20K
     - Web presence MVP €20K
   * **Suggested KPIs:**
     1. Number of artworks onboarded to licensing platform (target: 1,000 by Q2 2025)
     2. Monthly licensing revenue (target: €15,000/month by Q3 2025)
     3. Artist participation rate (target: 80% of represented artists with at least 10 works)
