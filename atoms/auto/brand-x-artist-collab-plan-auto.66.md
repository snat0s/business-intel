This prevents scenarios where the art could be distorted or used in a manner that the artist feels is inappropriate. Balancing this, brands may need flexibility for production practicalities, so delineating what requires artist approval (e.g. any alteration of the art itself) is key. Clear creative control clauses help avoid conflicts and ensure the artist’s rights over their creation are respected even in a commercial setting.
