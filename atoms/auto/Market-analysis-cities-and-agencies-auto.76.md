The city has a strong design sensibility and appreciation for illustration – Dutch graphic design often incorporates illustration. **Industries:** Advertising (Amsterdam agencies often handle international campaigns that require high-end illustration, e.g., for beer, electronics, and travel brands), **branding and design** (many design studios in the Netherlands use custom illustration for packaging and identity work), and **publishing** (while not as large as London or NY, Amsterdam has some publishing, especially art book and editorial publishing that uses illustrators). *Notable agencies:* **Agent Pekka’s European office is in Amsterdam**, drawn by the mix of global advertising agencies and design-savvy culture. Also, **Shop Around** is a prominent Amsterdam-based illustration agency representing local and international talent. **Illustration Daily** (an online platform) and the **Dutch Illustrators Guild (Illustratoren Nederland)** are based in NL, supporting the trade. Amsterdam’s creative reputation (“Dutch Design”) helps illustrators – clients often seek out Dutch illustrators for their distinct style. Many UK and US agencies also travel to Amsterdam for business since so many global brand HQs or marketing departments are there (e.g., Adidas, though a German company, has design teams in Amsterdam that commission illustration).
