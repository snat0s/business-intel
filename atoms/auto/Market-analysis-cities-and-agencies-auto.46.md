In addition, the market for commissioned illustrations (custom artwork created on order) contributes many billions more. One analysis of just the agency-brokered portion of commissions puts it at $16.5 billion in 2023, with healthy growth projected. Considering commissions done directly between clients and freelance illustrators (outside of agencies) on top of that, the total commissioned illustration spending worldwide is even higher.
