### Capital Requirements Risk
**Description**: Expansion and new initiatives may require more capital than anticipated.  
**Impact**: High | **Likelihood**: Medium | **Rating**: High  
**Mitigation Strategies**:
- Develop detailed financial models with conservative assumptions
- Create phased funding plan with clear milestones
- Identify multiple potential funding sources
- Establish capital reserves for contingencies
- Develop fallback plans for scaled-back expansion if funding is limited
