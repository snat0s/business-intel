Agent Pekka also fostered a sense of community and mentorship in the illustration world. Because they represent both **established names and emerging talent** ([Agent Pekka](https://www.gosee.news/agentpekka/page/about#:~:text=We%E2%80%99re%20dedicated%20to%203D%2C%20animation%2C,diverse%20as%20they%20are%20distinctive)), the agency often pairs younger illustrators with experienced agents to guide their careers. This practice has contributed to a new generation of illustrators learning how to navigate the global market. In industry circles, Pablo Steffa (the co-founder and director) became known not just as an agent but as an advocate for the illustration art form, often speaking about building strong portfolios and the value of original work. Such advocacy work has had a positive effect on how illustration is perceived – as a collectable art and as a key element of brand storytelling – thereby slightly shifting the market to prize illustrator contributions more.
